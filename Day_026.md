## Vocabulary Focus (14 words)

1. **negotiate** (v.) - obtain or bring about by discussion
   - *We need to negotiate terms that benefit both parties.*
   - Replace: discuss → negotiate

2. **propose** (v.) - put forward for consideration
   - *I'd like to propose an alternative arrangement.*
   - Replace: suggest → propose

3. **compromise** (v.) - settle by mutual concession
   - *We're willing to compromise on the timeline.*
   - Replace: meet halfway → compromise

4. **concede** (v.) - admit something is true after denial
   - *I can concede that point whilst maintaining my position.*
   - Replace: admit → concede

5. **leverage** (v.) - use to maximum advantage
   - *We can leverage our market position in negotiations.*
   - Replace: use → leverage

6. **accommodate** (v.) - fit in with wishes or needs
   - *We'll accommodate your scheduling requirements.*
   - Replace: adjust for → accommodate

7. **stipulate** (v.) - demand or specify as part of agreement
   - *We must stipulate clear deliverables in the contract.*
   - Replace: require → stipulate

8. **clarify** (v.) - make statement less confused
   - *Let me clarify our position on pricing.*
   - Replace: explain → clarify

9. **establish** (v.) - set up on firm basis
   - *We need to establish mutual expectations upfront.*
   - Replace: set → establish

10. **secure** (v.) - obtain or achieve
    - *Our goal is to secure favorable terms.*
    - Replace: get → secure

11. **finalize** (v.) - complete or agree on finished version
    - *We should finalize the agreement by Friday.*
    - Replace: complete → finalize

12. **ratify** (v.) - sign or give formal consent
    - *Both parties will ratify the contract next week.*
    - Replace: approve → ratify

13. **expedite** (v.) - make action happen sooner
    - *We can expedite the approval process.*
    - Replace: speed up → expedite

14. **facilitate** (v.) - make action or process easier
    - *I'll facilitate discussions between both teams.*
    - Replace: help → facilitate

## Negotiation Strategies

### Preparation Phase
- Establish your objectives and priorities
- Clarify your walk-away point
- Research the other party's interests

### Key Negotiation Phrases
- "I'd like to propose a solution that..."
- "We're willing to compromise on..."
- "Let me clarify our position..."
- "How can we facilitate an agreement?"

## How to Use These Mind Maps

### **For Daily Study:**
1. **Choose one chapter** to focus on
2. **Review the mind map** to see vocabulary themes
3. **Study 10-15 words** from one branch
4. **Create sentences** using those words
5. **Practice in context** during the day

### **For Visual Learning:**
- **See connections** between related words
- **Understand themes** within each chapter
- **Group vocabulary** by concept
- **Track progress** by marking mastered branches

### **For Revision:**
- **Use as quick reference** before reading chapters
- **Test yourself** by covering words and recalling
- **Create your own** mind maps for deeper learning
- **Add examples** from your own experience

### **For Application:**
- **Pick one branch** per day to use actively
- **Practice all words** in that branch
- **Write emails** using those specific words
- **Have conversations** incorporating the vocabulary

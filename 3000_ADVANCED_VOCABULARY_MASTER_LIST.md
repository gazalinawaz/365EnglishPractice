# 3000 Advanced Conversational Vocabulary - Master List

**Purpose:** Replace simple words with sophisticated alternatives in everyday conversation.

**Daily Revision Plan:** 100 words/day = 30-day cycle

---

## Quick Reference Guide

**Common Word → Advanced Alternatives**

- **go** → navigate, proceed, venture, traverse, embark, depart
- **think** → contemplate, ponder, deliberate, reflect, consider
- **good** → exceptional, remarkable, outstanding, superior, exemplary
- **bad** → detrimental, adverse, unfavorable, problematic, suboptimal
- **say** → articulate, express, convey, state, declare, proclaim
- **make** → create, generate, produce, construct, fabricate
- **get** → obtain, acquire, procure, secure, attain
- **use** → utilize, employ, leverage, harness, exploit
- **show** → demonstrate, illustrate, exhibit, display, reveal
- **help** → assist, facilitate, aid, support, enable

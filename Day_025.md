## Vocabulary Focus (14 words)

1. **address** (v.) - deal with or speak to
   - *We must address stakeholder concerns immediately.*
   - Replace: deal with → address

2. **mitigate** (v.) - make less severe or serious
   - *Our priority is to mitigate the reputational damage.*
   - Replace: reduce → mitigate

3. **contain** (v.) - keep under control
   - *We need to contain the situation before it escalates.*
   - Replace: control → contain

4. **stabilize** (v.) - make or become stable
   - *Our goal is to stabilize operations within 48 hours.*
   - Replace: steady → stabilize

5. **reassure** (v.) - remove doubts and fears
   - *I want to reassure all stakeholders that we're responding.*
   - Replace: comfort → reassure

6. **communicate** (v.) - share or exchange information
   - *We'll communicate updates every two hours.*
   - Replace: inform → communicate

7. **coordinate** (v.) - organize different elements
   - *I'm coordinating our response across all departments.*
   - Replace: organize → coordinate

8. **prioritize** (v.) - designate as most important
   - *We must prioritize customer safety above all else.*
   - Replace: focus on → prioritize

9. **escalate** (v.) - increase in intensity
   - *We need to escalate this to senior leadership.*
   - Replace: raise → escalate

10. **mobilize** (v.) - organize for action
    - *We're mobilizing our crisis response team.*
    - Replace: activate → mobilize

11. **implement** (v.) - put into effect
    - *We'll implement our contingency plan immediately.*
    - Replace: execute → implement

12. **monitor** (v.) - observe and check progress
    - *We're monitoring the situation continuously.*
    - Replace: watch → monitor

13. **respond** (v.) - react to something
    - *We're responding swiftly to all inquiries.*
    - Replace: react → respond

14. **restore** (v.) - bring back to original state
    - *Our objective is to restore normal operations.*
    - Replace: fix → restore

## Crisis Communication Principles

### Immediate Response
- Address the situation promptly
- Communicate transparently
- Prioritize stakeholder safety
- Coordinate response efforts

### Key Crisis Phrases
- "We're addressing this situation with urgency..."
- "Our priority is to mitigate any impact..."
- "We're mobilizing all necessary resources..."
- "I want to reassure you that..."

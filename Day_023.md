## Vocabulary Focus (14 words)

1. **adapt** (v.) - adjust to different conditions
   - *We must adapt our communication style for international clients.*
   - Replace: change → adapt

2. **accommodate** (v.) - adjust to cultural preferences
   - *I'll accommodate different working styles in our team.*
   - Replace: adjust for → accommodate

3. **respect** (v.) - have due regard for feelings or rights
   - *We respect diverse cultural perspectives in our organization.*
   - Replace: honor → respect

4. **appreciate** (v.) - recognize full worth of cultural differences
   - *I appreciate the unique insights your background brings.*
   - Replace: value → appreciate

5. **embrace** (v.) - accept willingly
   - *We embrace diversity as a competitive advantage.*
   - Replace: accept → embrace

6. **integrate** (v.) - combine to form whole
   - *Let's integrate diverse perspectives into our strategy.*
   - Replace: combine → integrate

7. **navigate** (v.) - find way through cultural complexity
   - *I'm learning to navigate cultural nuances in negotiations.*
   - Replace: handle → navigate

8. **bridge** (v.) - make connection between different groups
   - *We need to bridge cultural gaps in our global team.*
   - Replace: connect → bridge

9. **foster** (v.) - encourage development of
   - *We foster an inclusive environment for all cultures.*
   - Replace: encourage → foster

10. **cultivate** (v.) - develop cultural awareness
    - *I'm cultivating understanding of Asian business practices.*
    - Replace: develop → cultivate

11. **demonstrate** (v.) - show cultural sensitivity
    - *She demonstrates exceptional cross-cultural competence.*
    - Replace: show → demonstrate

12. **acknowledge** (v.) - recognize cultural differences
    - *I acknowledge that communication styles vary across cultures.*
    - Replace: recognize → acknowledge

13. **facilitate** (v.) - make cross-cultural interaction easier
    - *I'll facilitate communication between our international offices.*
    - Replace: help → facilitate

14. **harmonize** (v.) - bring into consonance
    - *We harmonize different cultural approaches in our methodology.*
    - Replace: blend → harmonize

## Cultural Communication Insights

### High-Context vs. Low-Context Cultures
**High-Context (Japan, China, Middle East):**
- Indirect communication
- Relationship-focused
- Non-verbal cues important

**Low-Context (US, Germany, Scandinavia):**
- Direct communication
- Task-focused
- Explicit verbal messages

### British Professional Culture
- Formal yet understated
- Indirect criticism
- Emphasis on politeness
- Hierarchical respect

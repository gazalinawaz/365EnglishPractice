## Vocabulary Focus (14 words)

1. **assess** (v.) - evaluate nature, ability, or quality
   - *I need to assess the candidate's technical capabilities.*
   - Replace: evaluate → assess

2. **evaluate** (v.) - form idea of amount or value
   - *We'll evaluate all candidates against consistent criteria.*
   - Replace: judge → evaluate

3. **demonstrate** (v.) - clearly show existence or truth
   - *I'll demonstrate my problem-solving approach through examples.*
   - Replace: show → demonstrate

4. **articulate** (v.) - express fluently and coherently
   - *She articulated her career goals with impressive clarity.*
   - Replace: express → articulate

5. **elaborate** (v.) - develop or present in detail
   - *Could you elaborate on your leadership experience?*
   - Replace: explain more → elaborate

6. **illustrate** (v.) - explain by using examples
   - *Let me illustrate my approach with a specific project.*
   - Replace: show → illustrate

7. **highlight** (v.) - pick out and emphasize
   - *I'd like to highlight my relevant achievements.*
   - Replace: emphasize → highlight

8. **convey** (v.) - communicate or make known
   - *I want to convey my enthusiasm for this opportunity.*
   - Replace: communicate → convey

9. **ascertain** (v.) - find out for certain
   - *I'm trying to ascertain whether this role aligns with my goals.*
   - Replace: determine → ascertain

10. **probe** (v.) - physically explore or examine
    - *I'll probe deeper into their problem-solving methodology.*
    - Replace: investigate → probe

11. **gauge** (v.) - estimate or determine amount or level
    - *I'm trying to gauge the company culture through questions.*
    - Replace: measure → gauge

12. **qualify** (v.) - be entitled to particular benefit
    - *I believe I qualify for this senior position.*
    - Replace: meet requirements → qualify

13. **substantiate** (v.) - provide evidence to support
    - *I can substantiate my claims with specific metrics.*
    - Replace: prove → substantiate

14. **align** (v.) - place in correct relative positions
    - *My values align closely with your organization's mission.*
    - Replace: match → align

## Interview Strategies

### As a Candidate
**Preparation:**
- Assess the role requirements thoroughly
- Articulate your value proposition clearly
- Demonstrate achievements with examples
- Gauge cultural fit through questions

**Key Candidate Phrases:**
- "I'd like to highlight my experience in..."
- "Let me illustrate this with an example..."
- "I can substantiate that with specific results..."
- "My approach aligns with your objectives..."

### As an Interviewer
**Evaluation:**
- Assess technical and cultural fit
- Probe beyond surface-level answers
- Evaluate problem-solving approach
- Ascertain genuine motivation

**Key Interviewer Phrases:**
- "Could you elaborate on that experience?"
- "How would you assess this situation?"
- "Can you substantiate that claim?"
- "Let's probe deeper into your methodology..."
